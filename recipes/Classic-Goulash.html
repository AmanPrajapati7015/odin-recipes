Classic Goulash

This meaty goulash recipe is easy to make on the stove top but can also be done in a slow cooker. Inspired by Paula Deen's Bobby's Goulash.

This easy goulash recipe is quick, easy, and wonderfully satisfying.

ingredients

  • Onion
  • Beef
  • Tomato Products
  • Sauces and Seasoning
  • Macaroni

Steps

  1. Preheat a slow cooker on High.
  2. Make the recipe through Step 1.
  3. Place browned beef and onion mixture into the preheated slow cooker.
  4. Mix in water, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, soy sauce, Italian seasoning, seasoned salt, and bay leaves.
  5. Add uncooked macaroni and stir until thoroughly combined.
  6. Cook on High for 1 hour.
